Dear Robert Furzer,

Our records indicate that some or all of your Remotely Piloted Aircraft (RPA) are due for registration renewal.

To renew your registration please login to myCASA and complete the following steps:

  1. Review and confirm RPA details
  2. Pay your registration fee
  3. Download your registration certificate

Please note CASA no longer accepts 'Other' as a model name for all DJI, Yuneec and Parrot drones. You will need to select a new model for this drone before it can be renewed. CASA will no longer issue serial number/registration marks for all DJI, Yuneec and Parrot drones. You will need to provide the correct serial number for these drones before they can be renewed.
